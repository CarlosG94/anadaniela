Hector Ramirez 

Dr. Hector Ramirez began working at the veterinary clinic at the age of 10 by accompanying his father to work every day. After his father's death he ran the business alognside his mother until 1990 after graduating from veterinary school. Currenlty hes been studying Opthalmology for 5 years and has receieved 5 diplomas. He is a current memeber of the Veterinary Opthalmology of the World Association and President of the AMMVEPE association.
