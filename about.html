Veterinaria Ramirez 

Veterinaria Ramirez was first established in 1966 by Hector Ramirez Bartel in Ciudad Juarez,  Chihuahua, Mexico. The first location of the clinic was established near the border crossing called Santa Fe Bridge, and now its current location is in Campestre Juarez. The clinic has had a total of 4 different locations and has expanded over the years to develop into one of the biggest veterinary clinics in Ciudad Juarez. The clinic has treated a wide variety of animals such as different breeds of dogs, cats, horses, cows, hamsters, and even exotic pets such as tigers and lions. Veterinaria Ramirez has come to symbolized a well known and trusted business recognized for quality, dedication, and passion for which its patients are taken care of.  The veterinary has been passed down through three generations to know be currently managed by Hector Angel Ramirez and continue to leave its mark on the community of Ciudad Juarez.
